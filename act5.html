Act V — Reaper of Souls

Dark gothic zones and the final battle for humanity

Quests

The Fall of Westmarch → Souls of the Dead → The Harbinger → The Witch → Pandemonium → Malthael (Final Boss)

Step-by-Step

1. Explore Westmarch
2. Push through undead zones
3. Defeat Urzael
4. Defeat Adria
5. Enter Pandemonium Fortress
6. Defeat Malthael

Enemy Warnings

High-density undead enemies
Fast melee attackers
Ranged threats with burst damage
Elite-heavy encounters

Duo Strategy

Monk controls large enemy packs
Witch Doctor uses AoE and pets
Stay grouped to avoid being overwhelmed

Mini Boss — Urzael

Key Mechanics:
- Fire attacks and explosions
- Ranged bombardment

Strategy:
- Stay mobile
- Avoid fire zones

Common Mistakes:
- Standing in fire
- Ignoring ranged attacks

Major Boss — Adria

Key Mechanics:
- Blood magic attacks
- Area denial zones

Strategy:
- Keep moving
- Avoid ground effects

Common Mistakes:
- Standing in damage zones
- Ignoring positioning

Final Boss — Malthael

Key Mechanics:
- Soul drain attacks
- Teleport and burst damage
- Multi-phase fight

Strategy:
- Stay mobile
- Manage positioning carefully
- Focus survival in later phases

Common Mistakes:
- Standing still too long
- Getting caught in burst damage
- Panicking during phase transitions
