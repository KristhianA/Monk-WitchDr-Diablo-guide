Act IV — The Prime Evil

Heavenly battleground and final confrontation

Quests

Fall of the High Heavens → The Light of Hope → Beneath the Spire → Diablo (Final Boss)

Step-by-Step

1. Enter the High Heavens
2. Push through angelic/demonic zones
3. Defeat Izual
4. Ascend the Crystal Arch
5. Defeat Diablo

Enemy Warnings

High burst damage enemies
Elite-heavy encounters
Tight combat spaces
Angelic enemies with strong ranged attacks

Duo Strategy

Monk controls positioning and survives bursts
Witch Doctor deals sustained ranged damage
Stay together to avoid getting overwhelmed

Major Boss — Izual

Key Mechanics:
- Frost attacks that slow movement
- Heavy melee swings
- Sustained pressure over time

Strategy:
- Stay mobile to avoid being slowed
- Keep distance when possible
- Focus steady damage instead of bursts

Common Mistakes:
- Standing still during attacks
- Letting slow effects stack
- Overcommitting to melee range

Final Boss — Diablo

Key Mechanics:
- Shadow Realm phase (isolation mechanic)
- Clones and high burst attacks
- Fire and lightning abilities
- Multi-phase transitions

Strategy:
- Learn attack patterns and timing
- Stay calm during Shadow Realm phase
- Focus survival over damage during transitions
- Regroup quickly after phase changes

Common Mistakes:
- Panicking during Shadow Realm
- Standing in fire/lightning zones
- Ignoring clones
- Overextending during transitions
