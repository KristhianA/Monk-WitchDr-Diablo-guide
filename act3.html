Act III — The Siege of Bastion's Keep

Battlefields, sieges, and large-scale demon warfare

Quests

The Siege of Bastion's Keep → Turning the Tide → The Breached Keep → Tremors in the Stone → Machines of War → Heart of Sin → The Prime Evil (Final Boss)

Step-by-Step

1. Defend Bastion’s Keep
2. Push through battlefield zones
3. Defeat Ghom
4. Defeat Siegebreaker
5. Enter Heart of Sin
6. Defeat Azmodan

Enemy Warnings

Large demon packs
Mortar/projectile enemies
Heavy melee elites
High damage bursts in tight areas

Duo Strategy

Monk groups enemies and controls space
Witch Doctor uses AoE and pets for pressure
Stay together — enemies overwhelm if split

Mini Boss — Ghom

Key Mechanics:
- Poison gas fills the room
- Sustained damage over time

Strategy:
- Keep moving constantly
- Avoid standing in poison clouds

Common Mistakes:
- Staying in gas too long
- Not repositioning

Major Boss — Siegebreaker Assault Beast

Key Mechanics:
- Charge attacks
- Grab + slam
- High burst damage

Strategy:
- Stay mobile
- Avoid direct hits
- Reposition after grabs

Common Mistakes:
- Standing in front of boss
- Getting caught by charge

Final Boss — Azmodan

Key Mechanics:
- Fire pools
- Laser beams
- Summoned enemies

Strategy:
- Avoid fire zones
- Stay spread
- Focus damage during safe windows

Common Mistakes:
- Standing in fire
- Ignoring adds
