Mini Boss — The Warden

Heavy hitter guarding access to the final boss

Overview

The Warden is a tough mid-to-late Act I encounter before the Butcher.

Mechanics

Heavy melee damage
High durability
Sustained pressure

Strategy

Take the fight slow and avoid overcommitting to damage.

Duo Strategy

Monk absorbs damage, Witch Doctor maintains safe distance and damage output.
