Final Boss — The Butcher

Act I’s final challenge — movement, awareness, and survival

Overview

The Butcher is the final boss of Act I and a major difficulty spike.

Core Mechanics

Charge Attack: Heavy damage rush

Hook Pull: Pulls players into danger

Fire Chains: Restrict movement

Arena Pressure: Becomes harder over time

Strategy

Stay moving constantly, avoid edges, prioritize survival over damage.

Duo Strategy

Monk controls positioning, Witch Doctor deals safe damage.

Common Mistakes

Standing still, panic movement, ignoring fire zones.
