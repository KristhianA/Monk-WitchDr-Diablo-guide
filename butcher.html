Final Boss — The Butcher

Act I’s final challenge — movement, awareness, and survival

Overview

The Butcher is the final boss of Act I and a major difficulty spike. This fight tests movement, positioning, and your ability to react under pressure.

Core Mechanics

Charge Attack: Rushes forward dealing heavy damage

Hook Pull: Pulls players toward him — dangerous if near fire

Fire Chains: Sections of the floor ignite and restrict movement

Enrage Pressure: Arena becomes harder to navigate over time

Fight Strategy (Step-by-Step)

1. Stay moving at all times
2. Avoid edges — they trap you with fire
3. Watch for charge direction
4. Reset positioning after every attack
5. Focus survival over damage

Duo Strategy (Monk + Witch Doctor)

Monk:
- Stay close to control boss movement
- Use healing abilities frequently
- Tank safely without overcommitting

Witch Doctor:
- Stay mid-range (not too far)
- Use pets as distractions
- Deal steady damage between mechanics

Common Mistakes

- Standing still too long
- Getting trapped by fire chains
- Panicking and running into danger
- Over-focusing damage instead of survival
