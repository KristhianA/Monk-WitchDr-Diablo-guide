Core Systems Acts Bosses
Act II

Mini Boss — Maghda

Overview

Maghda is a recurring Act II boss who relies on shields and summoned enemies to stall and overwhelm players.

Mechanics

Shield phases (invulnerable)
Summons minions
Projectile magic attacks

Strategy

Clear adds quickly and wait out shield phases. Focus damage when she becomes vulnerable.

Duo Strategy

Monk handles adds and pressure
Witch Doctor clears waves and deals safe ranged damage

Common Mistakes

Attacking during shield phase
Ignoring summoned enemies
Standing still too long
