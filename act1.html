Major Boss — Skeleton King

Skeleton King — Boss Arena

Key Mechanics:
- Teleport slam attack
- Summons skeleton adds

Strategy:
- Monk stays close and heals
- Witch Doctor stays back and deals damage
- Focus boss between summon phases

Common Mistakes:
- Standing still during slam
- Ignoring adds

Mini Boss — Jondar

Key Mechanics:
- Fast melee attacks
- High burst damage

Strategy:
- Monk engages first
- Witch Doctor stays back

Mini Boss — The Warden

Key Mechanics:
- Heavy melee attacks
- High health pool

Strategy:
- Monk tanks and controls positioning
- Witch Doctor keeps distance

Final Boss — The Butcher

Key Mechanics:
- Charges across the room
- Fire chains block movement
- Floor becomes hazardous

Strategy:
- Keep moving constantly
- Monk stays near boss for control
- Witch Doctor avoids edges and uses pets

Common Mistakes:
- Getting trapped by chains
- Standing in fire zones
