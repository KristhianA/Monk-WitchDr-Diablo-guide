Skeleton King — Boss Guide

Full mechanics, strategy, and duo play tips

Overview

The Skeleton King is the first major boss of Act I. He introduces mechanics that require movement, awareness, and positioning.

Mechanics

Teleport Slam: Appears near player and deals heavy damage

Summon Skeletons: Calls adds to overwhelm players

Melee Attacks: High damage if standing still

Duo Strategy (Monk + Witch Doctor)

Monk:
- Stay close to boss
- Use healing abilities
- Control positioning

Witch Doctor:
- Stay at range
- Use pets to tank
- Focus damage between summon phases

Common Mistakes

- Standing still during teleport attacks
- Ignoring skeleton adds
- Splitting too far apart
